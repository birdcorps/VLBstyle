Dreaming of books for holiday or birthday gifts, or for your school or other organization? You can create a wish list easily through Village Light’s website and have your friends and contacts purchase your books from your local independent bookstore!

Log in with your user name and password to begin. You can add any product from our online store to your wish list by clicking the “Add to Wish List” button on the product's page. Then click on "My Account" on the menu bar and choose the "Wish List" tab to customize your wish list or send it to your email contacts. Only those you choose will be able to see your wish list. (Please note: Village Lights employees cannot access your wish list in the store.)

May your every wish come true!
